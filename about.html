About GIDMAS PRIME

This is what we are building: the royal academy in your pocket.

GIDMAS PRIME pulls together everything a learner actually needs — a patient AI tutor named Sir Gideon, syllabus-aligned notes and exercises, video masterclasses, and a dashboard parents can trust. It is framed as one companion: serious about results, gentle in tone, unmistakably built for Botswana and the exams that matter here.

What you get inside

  • Sir Gideon — step-by-step help in language that feels human, tuned to PSLE, JCE, BGCSE and IGCSE.
  • A real library — simplified notes, worked examples and practice that follows the curricula you already sit for.
  • Lessons on demand — masterclasses you can watch when data allows, designed with offline-first families in mind.
  • Community — study circles where questions and tips travel between peers in a moderated space.
  • For parents — clarity on progress, strengths and gaps without jargon or overwhelm.
  • Extras — revision bundles and memberships that match how families actually pay locally.

Why “for Botswana”

This is not a generic global product with a palette swap. Pricing, curricula, subject names and contexts are anchored in Botswana’s classrooms and exams — PSLE through BGCSE, plus Cambridge IGCSE where families choose that route. Excellence is universal; the path to it here should speak your language.
